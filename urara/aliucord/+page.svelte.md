> Where can I download BetterDiscord for Android?

Back in early 2021, not many Discord mods existed for phone users.
Despite  being saturated with mods such as BetterDiscord or PowerCord, such luxuries were not
available to phone users. The only choices I still remember that existed atm were:

- CutTheCord (*feature limited*)
- Treecord (*dead*-ish)
- Bluecord (*sketchy*)

All 3 of these worked by the same principle, that being smali patches.
